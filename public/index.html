What Is LiteCall App

  • LiteCall App provides free peer-to-peer 1:1 meetings
  • LiteCall App does not require any authentification
  • LiteCall App does not track location & user data
  • LiteCall App does not use cookies
  • All calls are secure with SSL encription
  • So it is private, secure and free
