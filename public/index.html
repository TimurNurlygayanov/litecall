What Is LiteCall

  • LiteCall provides free peer-to-peer 1:1 meetings
  • LiteCall does not require any authentification
  • LiteCall does not track location & user data
  • LiteCall does not use cookies
  • All calls are secure with SSL encription
  • So it is private, secure and free
